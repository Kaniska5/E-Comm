# Agentic AI Customer Support — E-Commerce Policies FAQ

## Shipping Policy

- Standard delivery: 3–5 business days (₹49 fee)
- Express delivery: 1–2 business days (₹149 fee)
- Free shipping on orders above ₹999
- Orders placed before 2 PM are dispatched the same day (Mon–Sat)
- No delivery on Sundays and national holidays

## Return Policy

- Items can be returned within 10 days of delivery
- Products must be unused, in original packaging, with all tags intact
- Non-returnable items: perishables, digital downloads, personalized products
- To initiate a return: go to "My Orders" → select item → click "Return"
- Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after pickup

## Refund Policy

- Refunds are issued to the original payment method
- UPI/Net Banking refunds: 2–3 business days
- Credit/Debit card refunds: 5–7 business days
- Wallet refunds: instant
- COD refunds: bank transfer within 7–10 business days
- Refunds ≤ ₹1000 are auto-approved; higher amounts require review

## Payment Methods

- UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, BHIM)
- Credit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex, Rupay)
- Debit Cards
- Net Banking (all major banks)
- Cash on Delivery (orders up to ₹10,000)
- EMI (available on orders above ₹3,000 on select cards)

## Coupons & Offers

- Coupon codes are applied at checkout in the "Apply Coupon" field
- Only one coupon can be used per order
- Coupons cannot be combined with cashback offers
- Expired coupons cannot be reactivated — contact support for alternatives
- Platform-wide sale discounts are applied automatically — no code needed

## Account & Security

- Password reset: go to Login → "Forgot Password" → check your email
- If your account is locked, wait 30 minutes or contact support
- Enable 2FA in Account Settings → Security for extra protection
- Never share your OTP or password with anyone, including our support staff

## Order Cancellation

- Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of placement if not yet dispatched
- To cancel: "My Orders" → select order → "Cancel Order"
- Once dispatched, cancellation is not possible — initiate a return instead
- Prepaid order cancellations are refunded within 5 business days

## Damaged / Wrong Item

- Report within 48 hours of delivery with photos
- We will send a replacement or process a full refund — your choice
- Do not use the item; keep it in original condition for pickup
